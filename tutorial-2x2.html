How to solve a 2x2 cube

If you know how to solve a three by three you pretty much already know how to solve a two by two! 🧊
On a two by two you use the exact algorithms in a three by three but easier (it’s actually just a 3x3 but only corners.)
Let's check out the introduction video:
If you don’t know what’s the sexy move, it’s just a really really famous algorithm on Rubik’s cubes! 💫

Finding the White Corner 🔍

We first find a white corner. Once we find the white corner we then could determine that it’s the white side.
Like in this photo, I found a white corner here so I could determine that it’s gonna be the white side (about to be the white side in the future).
Only Corners!
Since there are no center or edge pieces on a 2x2 cube, any corner can be used to start the white side!

White Side for 2x2 ⚪

After we find a random corner we would want to solve the corner with the sexy move just like on the 3x3 for first layer as well!
Some people will think 🤔 why don’t us have daisy and cross anymore? It’s because the 2x2 is only corners, no edges, no centres only corners, and you might notice the daisy and the cross is edge business.
💡 TIP:

You should keep practicing until you could solve it blind folded for at least once or getting color neutral which means being able to solve from any side if you want to get fast and cook when you go to a competition. 🍳

Yellow side (OLL) for 2x2 🟡

You do the same as a 3x3, you also hold the yellow side on the bottom which means you put the white on the top like this, then do the same thing you would do on a three by three which is solving the yellow side with the sexy move again.

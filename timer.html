💡 FMC Beginner Guide

FMC (Fewest Moves Challenge) is about finding the shortest solution in 1 hour.

  • EO (Edge Orientation): Orienting edges makes the rest of the solve much easier. Count bad edges on the Front/Back faces.
  • Blockbuilding: Don't do cross + F2L! Try to build a 2x2x1 corner-edge block, then expand it to a 2x2x2 block.
  • NISS (Normal-Inverse Switch): If you get stuck, apply your moves to the cube, then swap to the Inverse Scramble to solve the remaining pieces!
  • Learn Skeletons & insertions: Solve the corners/edges, leaving 3 corners unsolved, and insert a 3-cycle commutator to finish.

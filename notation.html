Cube Notation Guide 📖

1. How to hold your cube when scrambing 👐

Default WCA Orientation:
Hold the cube so that the Green face is in front (facing you) and the White face is on top (facing the ceiling). Under this standard:
  • Yellow is on the bottom (facing the floor).
  • Red is on the right.
  • Orange is on the left.
  • Blue is on the back (facing away from you).

All scramble instructions assume you keep the cube in this exact position without rotating the whole cube in your hands while turning the layers!

3. Scramble Symbol Cheat Sheet 📋

Each letter represents a face. The symbols tell you which direction and how many times to turn that face.

Symbol Action name How to turn
R Right Face (Clockwise) Turn the Right layer UP (away from you).
R' Right Face Prime Turn the Right layer DOWN (towards you).
L Left Face (Clockwise) Turn the Left layer DOWN (towards you).
L' Left Face Prime Turn the Left layer UP (away from you).
U Up / Top Face (Clockwise) Turn the Top layer to the LEFT.
U' Up Face Prime Turn the Top layer to the RIGHT.
D Down / Bottom Face (Clockwise) Turn the Bottom layer to the RIGHT.
D' Down Face Prime Turn the Bottom layer to the LEFT.
F Front Face (Clockwise) Turn the Front layer like turning a steering wheel to the right.
F' Front Face Prime Turn the Front layer to the left.
B Back Face (Clockwise) Turn the Back layer (the side facing away).
B' Back Face Prime Turn the Back layer counter-clockwise.
U2 / R2 / F2... Double Turn Turn that face twice (180 degrees). Direction doesn't matter!
Slice Moves (Center Layers) 🍰
M / M' Middle Slice Turn the middle layer between L and R. **M** goes down (with L), **M'** goes up (with R).
E / E' Equator Slice Turn the middle layer between U and D. **E** goes right (with D), **E'** goes left (with U).
S / S' Standing Slice Turn the middle layer between F and B. **S** goes clockwise (with F), **S'** goes counter-clockwise.
Wide Moves (Two Layers together) 🍔
Rw / Lw / Uw... Wide Face Turns Turn the outer face **and** the adjacent middle slice together (2 layers at once).
r / l / u... Lowercase Notation Same as **Rw / Lw / Uw**! In modern notation, lowercase letters represent turning 2 layers.
